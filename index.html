About AcademeForge

Built for the whole student.

AcademeForge began in January 2024 as a Telegram community for Class 10 students — a space to solve doubts, take quizzes, and support each other. Today it is a practical learning ecosystem where students study, track progress, test knowledge, connect with peers, and manage their time. Affordable courses, an AI mentor, gamification, and productivity apps — all in one place.

Companion Apps · Beyond the Classroom

Beyond the Classroom

AcademeForge is not just courses. Timezy helps you manage time. Zenopulsky publishes honest journalism. Capacity helps you check in emotionally with a small trusted circle. None of them require payment. They are part of the ecosystem because student wellbeing and productivity matter as much as the curriculum.

Timezy 100% Free

Timezy — Time Management

A standalone productivity web app. Add tasks with title, description, priority level (High / Medium / Low), and due date. Plan your week visually — a calendar highlights busy and free days at a glance. A live progress ring fills as tasks are marked done. Supports dark and light mode. No account or download needed.

Zenopulsky Independent

Zenopulsky — Independent Journalism

AcademeForge's content publishing arm. Positioned as "Independent · Unfiltered · Necessary," Zenopulsky publishes long-form articles and journalism on Social Justice, Mental Health, Labour Rights & Gig Economy, Technology, Healthcare, and issues specific to India.

Capacity Private

Capacity — Private Wellbeing

A quiet, intentionally small app for sharing how you are doing with a trusted circle. Set your emotional state — Good, Okay, Stretched, Need Space, or Off the Grid — and your close group sees it. Includes a private group chat. Completely separate from the Community feed. Low pressure, honest, and private by design.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is AcademeForge?

+

AcademeForge is a growing Indian EdTech platform combining text-based Explore courses with instant certificates, LearnSpace video programs, the AF ProGuide AI mentor, gamified AF Test Arena quizzes with XP and global leaderboards, a Learning Heatmap for streak tracking, an in-app student Community, and free companion apps Timezy, Zenopulsky, and Capacity.

Do Explore courses give real certificates?

+

Yes. All Explore text-based courses issue a completion certificate instantly. Certificates are tracked in your personal Dashboard inside the AcademeForge app and can be verified at /verify-Certificate/.

Is AcademeForge beginner friendly?

+

Absolutely. Both Explore courses and LearnSpace programs are designed for beginners and students who want structure and practical guidance. AF ProGuide AI can also answer basic questions at any level, any time.

What is AF ProGuide and is it free?

+

AF ProGuide is AcademeForge's built-in AI student mentor. It runs multiple AI model versions — AF 2.4, Dev 1.3, and Tapri 7.9 — and helps students with learning to code, building a portfolio, preparing for interviews, and choosing a tech career. A free tier is available.

What is Timezy and does it cost anything?

+

Timezy is a 100% free browser-based productivity app by AcademeForge. No account or download required. Add tasks, plan your week visually, and track completion with a live progress ring. Supports dark and light mode.

When are LearnSpace fees and start dates announced?

+

LearnSpace video program fees and start dates are yet to be announced. They will be published once the platform structure, admission policies, and batch design are finalized. Watch the Community and Telegram group for official announcements.

How is student data used?

+

Student data may be used for account access, login support, batch allocation, class communication, assignment tracking, certificate verification, support tickets, safety monitoring, analytics, and platform improvement. Data is not sold to advertisers.
