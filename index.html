FAQ

Questions students ask first.

Are admissions open right now?

+

No. Admissions are currently paused while AcademeForge rebuilds the platform, program structure, policies, and learning experience.

Is AcademeForge beginner friendly?

+

Yes. Programs are planned for beginners and students who need structure and practical guidance. All 7 skill tracks start from fundamentals.

What is AF Nexus?

+

AF Nexus is AcademeForge's built-in student community. It includes a social Feed where students post updates, like and comment on posts, and read announcements — and a private Chat system with direct messages, pending requests, and a follow-to-chat permission model. It replaces the need for external Telegram groups.

What is LearnSpace?

+

LearnSpace is AcademeForge's self-paced learning module system. Unlike live batches that follow a fixed schedule, LearnSpace lets students access structured course content anytime and progress at their own speed. Students can track completion and earn certificates through LearnSpace independently.

What AI tools are available in the app?

+

The AcademeForge student app includes: an AI Assistant for academic and platform questions, Smart Search to find content instantly, AF Test Arena for MCQ practice, Revision Notes for quick recall, Brain Games like Sudoku for cognitive engagement, and a Learning Heatmap to visualize study habits.

What is the Test Portal?

+

The AF Test Arena is a built-in MCQ quiz system inside the student app. Students take timed tests and receive instant results with rank, score, and accuracy. After each test, review mode shows correct and wrong answers per question. Leaderboards display ranking among all batch students.

What is the daily streak?

+

The streak counter tracks how many consecutive days a student uses the app. It syncs server-side and shows in the app topbar. Brain Games also count toward maintaining your streak on days you don't have coursework — keeping the habit alive.

Will students get certificates?

+

Yes. Certificates may be issued for selected programs and LearnSpace tracks after completion requirements are met. Certificates are verifiable online via the AcademeForge certificate portal.

Does the app work offline?

+

Yes. The AcademeForge student app uses a Service Worker for offline caching. Cached pages and assets load without internet. A dedicated offline page shows when a student needs to reconnect for live features like tests or notifications.

How is student data used?

+

Student data may be used for account access, support, progress tracking, communication, security, analytics, and platform improvement. Data is not sold to advertisers.
