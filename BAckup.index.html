About AcademeForge

Built for the whole student.

AcademeForge began in January 2024 as a Telegram community for Class 10 students — a space to solve doubts, take quizzes, and support each other. Today it is a practical learning ecosystem where students study, track progress, test knowledge, connect with peers, and manage their time. Affordable courses, an AI mentor, gamification, and productivity apps — all in one place.

AcademeForge Scholars Test · AST

Scholars Test — Official Examination Portal

The AcademeForge Scholars Test (AST) is an official examination platform for school students. Students register through their school, receive an official admit card with a unique Roll Number, and appear for the test through the dedicated AST portal. The examination is structured, school-code based, and designed to assess academic readiness across classes and tiers.

🎓

Official Admit Card

Every registered student receives an official AcademeForge Scholars Test admit card with a unique Roll Number (e.g., AST-2026-0001), class tier, and school code.

🏫

School-Based Registration

Students register through their school using a unique School Code. Registration is coordinated by the school administrator or authorised AcademeForge representative.

📊

Class-Wise & Tier-Based

The AST is structured by class (e.g., Class VIII) and tier (Standard or Premium), ensuring the examination is appropriately calibrated for every student's level.

🔒

Secure & Official

The AST portal is secure and accessible only to registered students with valid credentials. Impersonation and unauthorised access are strictly prohibited.

📄

Results & Recognition

Results are published through official AcademeForge channels. Top performers receive recognition and merit acknowledgement from the platform.

📱

Accessible on Any Device

The AST portal is fully responsive and accessible on mobile, tablet, and desktop. No additional download is required — just a browser and valid login.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is AcademeForge?

+

AcademeForge is a growing Indian EdTech platform combining text-based Explore courses with instant certificates, LearnSpace video programs, the AF ProGuide AI mentor, gamified AF Test Arena quizzes with XP and global leaderboards, the AcademeForge Scholars Test (AST) examination portal, a Learning Heatmap for streak tracking, an in-app student Community, and free companion apps Timezy, Zenopulsky, and Capacity.

Do Explore courses give real certificates?

+

Yes. All Explore text-based courses issue a completion certificate instantly. Certificates are tracked in your personal Dashboard inside the AcademeForge app and can be verified at /verify-Certificate/.

Is AcademeForge beginner friendly?

+

Absolutely. Both Explore courses and LearnSpace programs are designed for beginners and students who want structure and practical guidance. AF ProGuide AI can also answer basic questions at any level, any time.

What is AF ProGuide and is it free?

+

AF ProGuide is AcademeForge's built-in AI student mentor. It runs multiple AI model versions — AF 2.4, Dev 1.3, and Tapri 7.9 — and helps students with learning to code, building a portfolio, preparing for interviews, and choosing a tech career. A free tier is available. Access it at ai.academeforge.in.

What is the AcademeForge Scholars Test (AST)?

+

The AcademeForge Scholars Test (AST) is an official examination platform for school students. Students register through their school using a unique School Code, receive an official admit card with a unique Roll Number, and appear for the test at ast.academeforge.in.

What is Timezy and does it cost anything?

+

Timezy is a 100% free browser-based productivity app by AcademeForge. No account or download required. Add tasks, plan your week visually, and track completion with a live progress ring. Supports dark and light mode.

When are LearnSpace fees and start dates announced?

+

LearnSpace video program fees and start dates are yet to be announced. They will be published once the platform structure, admission policies, and batch design are finalized. Watch the Community and Telegram group for official announcements.

How is student data used?

+

Student data may be used for account access, login support, batch allocation, class communication, assignment tracking, certificate verification, support tickets, safety monitoring, analytics, and platform improvement. Data is not sold to advertisers.
